Sweet and Sour Ribs

The sweet and sour ribs is one of the first dishes I've learned how to make. It uses only 5 simple ingredients and is both tangy and sweet. You should try making it.

Ingredients:

  • Ribs- 1 lb
  • Vinegar- 4 tablespoons
  • Sugar- 3 tablespoons
  • Soy Sauce- 2 tablespoons
  • ShaoXin Wine- 1 tablespoon

Cooking Steps:

  1. Chop ribs into small pieces, roughly 1-2 inche cubes.
  2. Boil ribs in water for 10 minutes. During this time, remove 'floaties' that arise
  3. Remove ribs from pot and rinse Ribs well.
  4. In clean pan, heat oil and then fry ribs for 4 minutes
  5. Mix Vinegar, Sugar, Soy Sauce and ShaoXin Wine well and add to ribs
  6. Add about 1/4 cup of water.
  7. Turn heat to high and reduce til simmering, enjoy!
