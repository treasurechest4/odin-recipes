Pan Fried Brussels Sprouts

This dish is perfect for adding some greens into your diet. It is very simple to make and is both delicious and nutritious!

Ingredients:

  • Brussel Sprouts
  • Spring Onion
  • Chia Seeds/Any other seed or nuts

Cooking Steps:

  1. Clean your Brussels Sprouts and Halve them
  2. Heat up cast iron pan, add 2 tablespoons of oil
  3. Brussels Sprouts to pan face down and cook for 5 minutes
  4. Turn over Brussels sprouts and season with Salt and Pepper
  5. Close the lid and cook on other side for 3 minutes
  6. Open lid and add chopped spring onion and chia seeds, stirfry for 1 minute
