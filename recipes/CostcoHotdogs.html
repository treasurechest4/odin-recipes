Costco Hot Dogs

This is a staple for consumers of all ages. The price has been immortalized by the founder James Sinego's words to his board: "If you raise the effing hot dog, I will kill you."

Ingredients:

  • Costco Membership/Foreign Passport/Costco Gift Card
  • $1.50

Cooking Steps:

  1. Drive to Costco
  2. Show membership to door checker/obtain day pass at customer service using Passport/Gift Card
  3. Walk to food court area
  4. Purchase hot dog using digital till
  5. Pick up your hot dog when your number gets called
  6. It even comes with a drink, enjoy!
